Another selling point for Elm is the package [elm-ui](https://package.elm-lang.org/packages/mdgriffith/elm-ui/latest/) (with [this talk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ie-gqwSHQr0)) as an alternative to CSS. It actually allows you to center a div with `centerX` and `centerY` attributes! Also, Evan Czaplicki, the creator of Elm is also developing a new language for describing database tables and queries, which I keenly await.
